About

I am a PhD student at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, working in the field of educational psychology and early childhood education. My research focuses on kindergarten process quality, family engagement, family–school partnerships, and young children’s school readiness.

一名以教育理想为毕生信念的研究者与践行者。游走于中西教育语境， 深耕于教学一线与学术研究，始终致力于推动教育实践革新与教育质量提升。

自悉尼、墨尔本启程，辗转新加坡，最终回归故土；从中学教育、成人教育到学前教育， 我始终追寻优质教育的理想之光。于 Kindy Lab，我找到了将初心与使命落于实践的价值与力量。 每一次观察访谈、每一轮测评实践、每一份反馈建议，都让我愈发坚定初心： 携手同行，做有温度的研究，办有价值的教育。

Research

Research Areas

Kindergarten Process Quality

Investigating classroom interaction, instructional support, emotional climate, and learning opportunities in early childhood settings.

Family Engagement

Examining how home learning environments, parental involvement, and caregiving arrangements support children’s early development.

Family–School Partnership

Understanding how relationships between families and kindergartens create developmental affordances for children.

Child School Readiness

Studying emergent literacy, numeracy, executive function, and social-emotional competence as multidimensional developmental outcomes.

Educational Evaluation

Developing practical approaches to quality monitoring, feedback reports, and evidence-informed improvement in early childhood education.

AI-Assisted Assessment

Exploring how AI tools can support classroom observation, text analysis, feedback generation, and scalable quality evaluation.

Projects

Selected Projects

Longitudinal Study of Kindergarten Quality and Child Development

AI-Assisted Process Quality Monitoring in Early Childhood Education

A developmental profiling study investigating how caregiving arrangements relate to children’s school readiness patterns over time.
